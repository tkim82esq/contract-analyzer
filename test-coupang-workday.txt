SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Coupang Corp. ("Client") and Workday Inc. ("Service Provider").

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Service Provider shall provide cloud-based HR and financial management services to Client as described in Exhibit A.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Client agrees to pay Service Provider $500,000 annually. Payment due NET 30 days after invoice.

3. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
Service Provider guarantees 99% uptime. Credits apply for downtime exceeding this threshold.

4. DATA SECURITY
Service Provider shall maintain industry-standard security measures for all Client data.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information shared during this engagement.

6. TERM
This agreement shall commence on January 1, 2024 and continue for a period of three (3) years.

7. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate with 90 days written notice.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of California.

9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
